The synthesis workflow

Colleagues bring a question or topic. This hub draws simultaneously from two streams — live market signals (hyperscaler intelligence, investment data, field landscape) and research literature (academic evidence, grantee outputs, evidence-ladder grading) — and converges them into a single structured brief with a full provenance trail.

📊 Market stream — Market Insights

Live intelligence on what the field, market, and hyperscalers are doing — essential context before any investment or strategy decision.

  • Hyperscaler AI infrastructure signals
  • Investment opportunity & concentration briefs
  • Field landscape scans & emerging players
  • LMIC sovereignty & access flags
🫰 Literature stream — EDUAgent

Multi-step agentic synthesis across academic evidence, grantee reports, and field literature — graded on the six-rung evidence ladder.

  • Evidence synthesis across RCTs & meta-analyses
  • Evidence gap mapping
  • Citation chain traversal
  • Knowledge graph deep dive & investment calibration

Core Question
"When, for whom, and under what conditions does it work?"
EDUAgent treats variation across classrooms, schools, and student populations as signal, not noise — shortening the distance between evidence and action.
Component 1 — Evidence Ladder
What can be claimed at each rung
RungStageMay claim
1MonitoringUsage, uptake, feasibility
2ImplementationCan be implemented; fidelity
3ComparativeFavorable vs. comparison
4Causal EffectivenessCaused improvement in studied settings
5GeneralizabilityEffects hold across settings
6PersonalizedDifferential benefit for groups
